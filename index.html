Mon Apprentissage

A quiet place to think.

Topic:

I'll be answering some questions, regarding a particular topic that I briefly learn about, similar to those mentioned below:

  1. What did I encounter this week that genuinely stayed with me?
  2. What did I actually understand (not just read or hear)?
  3. What surprised me, disturbed me, or challenged my assumptions?
  4. How does this connect to something I already know?
  5. Did this change how I see something, even slightly?
  6. What questions did this leave me with?
  7. How does this reflect who I am becoming?
  8. One sentence to carry into the next week.
  9. Where did I feel stuck or avoidant this week?
